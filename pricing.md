# Pricing

Billed in USD through Shopify Billing. Annual billing on Grow and Unlimited is roughly two months free compared to paying monthly. Switch plans or cancel any time from **Pricing** inside the app — changes take effect immediately, no email required.

## Free — $0/month

- Catalog audit, first 20 products, one-time (no history)
- Crawler simulator, 20/month
- Competitor comparison, 10/month
- Organization schema, `llms.txt`, `agents.md` — no limit
- Content chunking score — no limit
- Storefront FAQ block, all 30 interface languages
- AI support chat, 1,000 messages/month

## Grow — $12.99/month ($129.90/year)

Everything in Free, plus:

- Full catalog audit, no product limit, with history
- Crawler simulator, 300/month
- Competitor comparison, 50/month
- AI FAQ generation, 500/month
- AI content rewriting for GEO, 300/month
- AI visibility tracking (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity), 5 tracked prompts
- AI referral traffic tracking

## Unlimited — $24.99/month ($249.90/year)

Everything in Grow, plus:

- Crawler simulator, 1,000/month
- Competitor comparison, 150/month
- AI visibility tracking, 15 tracked prompts
- MCP server for AI agent access (Claude Desktop and other MCP clients)

## Billing questions

**Is FAQ generation really capped at 500/month, even on Unlimited?**
Yes, deliberately — see [FAQ](faq.html) for why.

**Why is the AI support chat the same on every plan?**
Assistance is included for everyone, never a paid differentiator — same reasoning, see [FAQ](faq.html).

**Do prices ever change without notice?**
No. Shopify's Billing API requires merchant approval for any plan change before it takes effect.

Full plan-by-plan feature table and more billing questions: see [FAQ](faq.html#plan-limits).
