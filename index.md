IFG GEO Optimizer helps Shopify merchants make their product catalog easier for AI search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Overviews — to read, cite, and recommend. It's **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization), a different discipline from traditional SEO: structured data, machine-readable content, and crawler access matter more than keyword rankings.

This site has everything you need whether you're using the app, evaluating it, or reviewing it for the Shopify App Store.

## What the app does

- **Catalog audit** with one-click fixes for missing structured data (schema.org), plus `llms.txt` and `agents.md` generation and Organization schema.
- **Crawler simulator** — see exactly what an AI bot (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) sees on a product page, no JavaScript, including per-bot `robots.txt` checks.
- **Content chunking score** — how easy your product copy is for a generative engine to extract facts from, not just whether markup is present.
- **AI-assisted FAQ generation** and a **content rewrite** tool, both always reviewed and approved by you before anything publishes.
- **Link Coach** — internal linking analysis across your catalog.
- **Visibility AI tracking** — weekly checks of whether Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity mention your store, plus AI referral traffic measurement.
- **Competitor comparison** — the same GEO signals, side by side with a competitor's product page.
